About Me

Jan 12th, 2015 12:24 am

Hey there, thanks for stopping by. I am Prashanth Babu (aka P7h or PB), a Big Data developer; currently in London, UK.

I am a software engineer since 2002 and almost all the years worked on Java and related technologies mostly and for a brief while developed Android apps too.
Since 2011, I have been working on Big Data and Hadoop Ecosystem on various R & D initiatives at NTT DATA. I am a Cloudera Certified Developer for Apache Hadoop [CCDH]. One of my projects was showcased at Gartner Summit, Barcelona in 2013 and another was highlighted in NTT DATA website.

I use this space mostly to share my experiences, experiments and thoughts on coding, best practices, productivity tips and devops here; predominantly about Big Data, Hadoop Ecosystem, Java and Android.

Head to over my About Me profile page for a bit more info.

You are more than welcome to drop me a line to discuss anything. If you would like to reach out to me, please check my details on contact page on this website.

Disclaimer: All rants, views and opinions expressed here are solely mine and mine alone and do not necessarily represent my current or previous employers.
