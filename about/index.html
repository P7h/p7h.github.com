About Me

Jan 12th, 2015 12:24 am

Hey there, thanks for stopping by. I am Prashanth Babu (aka P7h or PB), a Big Data developer; currently in London, UK.

I am a software engineer since 2002; yeah more than 12 years. And since 2011, I have been working on Big Data and Hadoop Ecosystem on various R & D initiatives at NTT DATA. I am a Cloudera Certified Developer for Apache Hadoop [CCDH]. One of my projects was showcased at Gartner Summit, Barcelona in 2013 and another was highlighted in NTT DATA website.

I use this space to share my experiences, experiments and thoughts on coding, best practices, productivity tips and devops here; predominantly about Big Data, Hadoop Ecosystem, Java and Android.

About Me has a bit more info than shared on this page. In due time, I will try to add all that and more info here.

If you would like to reach out to me, please check my details on contact page and you are more than welcome to drop me a line to discuss anything.
